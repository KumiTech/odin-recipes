Waakye

Description

Ghanaian local delicacy with meat, fish ,gari and shito

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Beans(blacked-eyed peas)
  • Dried Sorghum Leaves
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Seasonings
  • Meat
  • Shito

Steps

  • Rinse the rice and beans, then soak them in water for about 30 minutes to an hour.
  • Wash and tie the waakye leaves in a bundle.
  • In a large pot, combine the soaked rice and beans, waakye leaves, salt, and water. Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat and simmer until the rice and beans are cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  • Remove the waakye leaves from the pot and discard.
  • Serve the waakye hot, often accompanied by various side dishes such as stews, meats, or vegetables.
