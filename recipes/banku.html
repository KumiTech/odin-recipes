Banku with Okro stew

Description

Local made banku and stew with fish, wale and crabs

Ingredients

  • Corn Dough
  • water
  • Okra
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Palm or Vegetable Oil
  • Fish(.eg tilapia or mackerel)
  • Crabs
  • seasonings
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Wale

Steps

Banku Preparation

  1. Mix corn dough or fermented corn dough with water to form a smooth, thick paste
  2. Gradually add more water while kneading until the dough becomes soft and pliable
  3. ook the dough in boiling water, stirring continuously until it forms a smooth, stretchy consistency
  4. Serve hot

Okro stew Preparation

  1. Chop okra into small pieces and blend with onions, peppers, and a little water
  2. Heat palm oil in a pot and saut√© onions until fragrant
  3. Add the blended okra mixture, salt, and other seasonings
  4. Cook until the okra thickens the stew to your desired consistency
  5. Add fish or meat (if using) and simmer for a few minutes

Serving

  1. Serve the banku with the okro stew
  2. Garnish with chopped fresh herbs or a squeeze of lemon juice, if desired
